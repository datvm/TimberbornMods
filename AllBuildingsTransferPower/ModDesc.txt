This mod simply makes all buildings, and all parts of a building, be able to transfer power.

Instant buildings (like Roads) are excluded, except the District Center. Power Shafts are also excluded because they are special buildings that would crash if modified.

[img]https://files.catbox.moe/o9cwm9.jpg[/img]

Intended to be for my Conveyor Belts mod to make power transfer less of a chore but it is not required.
