Beaver Chronicles Waterfall Event Pack adds map-specific story events for the Waterfalls map.

This is an expansion pack for [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3725316854]Beaver Chronicles[/url]. It requires Beaver Chronicles v11.0 or newer.

[h1]Waterfall Stories[/h1]

The Waterfalls map now has its own Chronicle Events:
- A different starting choice
- A suspiciously spicy hooman recipe
- Strange discoveries beneath the Badtide Drain and a dramatic sinkhole event can permanently reshape part of the map.

These events are designed around the Waterfalls terrain and will only appear on that map.

[h1]For Mod Developers[/h1]

This pack is also a JSON-event example pack. The events are written without custom C# event code and demonstrate map-specific events, follow-up chains, water-source buffs, entity searches, spawning, camera effects, and terrain-changing events.
