Building Decals came back to Timberborn V1 with a new name: Building Banners! Give your buildings some style: you can decorate any building in Timberborn with custom images. Flags, emblems, clan symbols, warning signs, memes - whatever you want! And best of all, you can put them directly on any building!

[img]https://files.catbox.moe/5nz3r5.jpg[/img]

- Add unlimited banners to any building: The mod uses the same source of images as the in-game banners so you can subscribe to wonderful banners mods on Steam Workshop and use them in your game, or use your own file! Works with any PNG file (supports transparency). You can even add holes or cutouts. Uses 256 PPU (Pixels Per Unit): a 256×256px image matches a 1×1 building, but you can freely scale them. 

[img]https://files.catbox.moe/mnbnqo.jpg[/img]

- Rotate, scale, and position banners anywhere. You can also Flip and Tint banners.
- Copy & paste banner setups between buildings.

Tips:
- Use [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3472334132]TImprove 4 UI[/url] for a better scrollbar.
- Use [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3547764728]TImprove 4 UX[/url] for collapsible panels.
- When sliders are tricky, hold Shift and click the number to enter values manually.

- Layer multiple banners for unique effects. You can also use transparency channel for glass effect. This is a good banner for making window glass thanks to @Anythin:

[img]https://files.catbox.moe/rlrdlt.jpg[/img]

The banners are purely visual and do not affect gameplay.

Be sure to share your feedback and awesome screenshot to me on official Discord https://discord.gg/timberborn, in the [url=https://discord.com/channels/558398674389172225/1417907482973442140]Building Banners[/url] channel!
