Building Renovations

Renovations for your buildings. Materials are delivered via Distro Storage (no builders/haulers).

Core engine only — individual renovations/upgrades will be added over time.

How materials work:
- Start a renovation on a finished building.
- The building becomes a Distro receiver for the required materials.
- Link Distro-enabled stockpiles in range so goods can be sent.
- Materials arrive gradually. Work time starts once all materials are collected.
- Canceling refunds delivered materials as recovered goods on the map.
- Completing a renovation consumes the materials (no refund).
- Destroying a building mid-renovation also refunds delivered materials.

Requires: Moddable Timberborn, Distro Storage.
