Configure the lifetime of beavers and bots as well as the growth of beavers children. Requested by someone.

By default this mod does not change anything. You need to confgure it: Menu (Main Menu or Game Menu) -> Mods -> Settings button next to the mod's name.

- Beaver Aging Multiplier: Adult Beavers' aging is multiplied by this value, similar to a "Life expectancy" bonus. Note: higher is harder as beavers will age faster. Setting to 0 means beavers will never age.
- Childhood Growth Multiplier: Children Beavers' childhood growth is multiplied by this value, similar to a "Growth speed" bonus. Note: higher is easier as young beavers will grow faster. Setting to 0 means children will never grow.
- Bot Deterioration Multiplier: Bots' deterioration is multiplied by this value, similar to a "Life expectancy" bonus. Note: higher is harder as bots will deteriorate faster. Setting to 0 means bots will never deteriorate.
