This mod adds Conveyor Belts and Lifts to the game, allowing you to transport items between buildings!

[i]Example: An IT Plank production:[/i]
[img]https://files.catbox.moe/oi3lst.jpg[/img]

The buildings are located in the Storages group.

[table]
[tr][th]Name[/th][th]Cost[/th][th]Power Usage[/th][th]Description[/th][/tr]
[tr][td]Conveyor Belt[/td][td]600 Science; 4 Planks, 4 Gears[/td][td]15 HP[/td][td]Transports items horizontally between two buildings.[/td][/tr]
[tr][td]Conveyor Belt Merger[/td][td]100 Science; 12 Planks, 12 Gears[/td][td]15 HP[/td][td]Merges items from multiple input conveyor belts into one output conveyor belt.[/td][/tr]
[tr][td]Conveyor Lift Up[/td][td]300 Science; 4 Planks, 8 Gears[/td][td]50 HP[/td][td]Lifts items to the building above.[/td][/tr]
[tr][td]Conveyor Lift Down[/td][td]300 Science; 4 Planks, 8 Gears[/td][td]5 HP[/td][td]Lowers items to the building below.[/td][/tr]
[tr][td]Smart Conveyor Belt[/td][td]750 Science; 4 Planks, 4 Gears, 2 Metal Blocks[/td][td]30 HP[/td][td]Transports items horizontally between two buildings and filters which goods are accepted.[/td][/tr]
[/table]

Belts and Lifts can grab items from buildings or other belts in the arrow directions, then drop them onto the next belt or building. Because you cannot build Lift Up above [b]Folktails[/b]' Piles, they cannot use lifts to bring Pile items upward, though they can still drop them downward with Lift Down built below the Pile. Iron Teeth belts, however, cannot transport [b]Liquids[/b] like Water, mostly for balance reasons.

[i]Example: A Centrifuge setup that is only possible with Folktails[/i]
[img]https://files.catbox.moe/hn2mc6.jpg[/img]

Conveyor Belt Mergers are special buildings. Technically, they are not even considered belts. They only work with other belts and lifts. A Merger has 5 inputs, so it can accept items from Lifts as well, but its only output direction is horizontal.

[h1]FAQS[/h1]
Q: Corner belt?
A: Build a small storage building or use Mergers.

Q: How do I lift Logs [or items stored in Pile]?
A: Folktails do not have any non-modded Solid building that can contain Logs, so you cannot. For Iron Teeth, simply build a Conveyor Lift Up above an Industrial Pile.

Q: Where's the splitter?
A: Build a storage building. Any side of a building can be used as a source.

Q: I still want a splitter!
A: Not for now. If there is enough outrage, I [b]may[/b] consider it.

Q: The warnings are annoying!
A: Use [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3635342825]Warnings Be Gone[/url] to hide certain warnings, such as no entrance, since you will probably have a lot of inaccessible storage buildings now.

Q: I accidentally got stuff on my belt and it's stuck!
A: Use the "Eject Content" button.

Q: My belt system is nice but Haulers and Workers still carry stuff manually.
A: Use [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3760937316]Disable Hauling[/url] if you prefer belts to do all the work.

Q: Power is annoying!
A: All belt buildings transmit power. You will figure it out! Use [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3414359638]Always Connected[/url] if you prefer not to deal with power connections. [spoiler]There may be a cheat coming later to disable Power for Belts completely.[/spoiler]

[b]Q: The mod does not show up?[/b]
A: This mod only works with Timberborn v1.1 (currently in the Exp branch).

Q: Your FAQ does not answer my frequently asked question!
A: Go to the official Discord. I am (likely) there.
