Adds 26 new achievements to Timberborn, including 7 secret ones to discover during gameplay. More achievements may be added over time based on suggestions and contributions from other modders.

Works for all players, including non-Steam versions, through integration with Moddable Timberborn Achievements, along with support for its extended achievement features.

Designed to expand long-term goals and add new challenges for both new and experienced settlements alike.
