Mark anything, anywhere.

Place colorful, customizable pins on the map to mark key locations. Adjust each pin's color, text, and height to your liking.

Found under the Decorations tab.
[i]'I don't remember why I put a pin here, I just know it's important.' — Anonymous[/i]

Open the [b]Pins Management Tab[/b] (default Shift + F1) to view and organize all your pins. You can click on any to select and go to its location.
[img]https://images.steamusercontent.com/ugc/11854738852058522721/4F7DFD13E5130EFEC8F74127264CA5FE4E57C55E/?imw=5000&imh=5000&ima=fit&impolicy=Letterbox&imcolor=%23000000&letterbox=false[/img]

Notes: Uses a Path model but doesn't function as a path.

Tip: Supports [url=https://docs.unity3d.com/Packages/com.unity.ugui@1.0/manual/StyledText.html]Unity Rich Text[/url] for styling — for example:
[code]
<color="red">Hello</color> <size=30>World</size>!
[/code]
