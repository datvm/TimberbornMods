Connect your buildings with invisible [b]Power Lines[/b] instead of bulky Power Shafts! This mod completely adds a new way to distribute power, giving you clean, flexible wiring between any power-using structures.

[h1]Main Features[/h1]
Each building can connect to up to 2 other buildings. Use the familiar connection UI (similar to Ziplines) to link and unlink them quickly. Power Lines are completely free to build and take no space. They do have a distance limit. Some buildings have special extended ranges, and the longer range always determines the maximum distance when connecting.


[h1]Repurposed Lantern[/h1]
The Lantern has been moved to the Power category. It is now a cheap, versatile power hub with more connection slots and longer range. Most production buildings are intentionally limited in range/connections so they are best used in daisy-chains, while Lanterns serve as natural relays and junctions.

[i]Some buildings can support custom and multiple connection points. The points are represented by the orange spheres:[/i]
[img]https://images.steamusercontent.com/ugc/11550511091467030764/01E67778DC8B64FF85B11F5F7011FE0DF707265B/?imw=5000&imh=5000&ima=fit&impolicy=Letterbox&imcolor=%23000000&letterbox=false[/img]

- The Clutch still works exactly as before with both Power Lines and Power Shafts.
- Power Shaft buildings are hidden from the toolbar as it is less useful now. You can still build them by [spoiler]enabling Dev Mode[/spoiler]. Existing Power Shafts will continue to work normally. Base game power transfer continues to work normally: buildings placed next to each other will still share power without needing a Power Line.
- Lanterns also transfer power at their base position. The Lantern's light now also serves as a visual indicator of your connected power network's efficiency.
