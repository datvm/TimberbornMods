When the skies themselves conspire, no dam can save you. The Skyblight & Badrain expansion for [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3630523180]Moddable Weather[/url] is back with even more challenges!

[img]https://files.catbox.moe/fvap3a.jpg[/img]

Both Skyblight and Badrain are now weather modifiers so you can combine it with other weather types. Skyblight is also a standalone weather type.

[h1]Skyblight[/h1]

Think you're safe with your massive reservoir that withstands the worst droughts? Now the air itself will turn against you: toxic pollen will slowly contaminate all water, bypassing traditional defenses.

[img]https://files.catbox.moe/44qzvk.jpg[/img]

This weather is enabled by default when you have this mod. Skyblight is now both a weather type and a modifier. [b]Note: by default, Skyblight is also enabled along with Badtide at 20% chance[/b].

- Water in storages is safe.
- Any outside water will gradually become contaminated. You can configure the strength of the effect. At default 10%/hour, it will takes 10 hours for your reservoir without any incoming fresh water to become fully contaminated.

[h1]Badrain[/h1]
If Skyblight isn't challenging enough, combine Badrain with it or other weather types. In this version, Badrain has even more effects. You can choose the effects and its strength in the settings:

[img]https://files.catbox.moe/5hb4w7.jpg[/img]

- Limit the moisture range of your land.
- Contaminate the land every now and then.
- Beavers get sick if outside for too long every day.
- Bots get damaged when outside.

It can be combined with almost any weather type except Rain.
