Bring 🇨🇦 Canada's beloved Timbits to Timberborn! Treat your beavers (and bots!) to these bite-sized delights, now with a mechanical twist for your bots.

Add [b]Timbits[/b] and [b]Timbots[/b] to the game for both Folktails and Iron Teeth.

[h1]Timbits[/h1]

This sweet treat gives your beavers gives [b]+4 Wellbeing[/b].

- Folktails: Make in the [b]Bakery[/b]: 1 Wheat Flour + 1 Maple Syrup + 1 Berries -> 2 Timbits.
- Iron Teeth: Make in the [b]Food Factory[/b]: 1 Canola Oil + 1 Corn + 1 Berries -> 2 Timbits.

Both recipes take [b]1.5 hours[/b] and use Log as fuel, 1 Log lasts 20 cycles.

[h1]Timbots[/h1]

This mechanical version gives your bots [b]+1 Boost[/b]. Both factions use the same recipe:

[b]1 Gear + 1 Pine Resin + 1 Extract -> 1 Timbot in 1.5 hours[/b]

The Folktails produce the Timbots in the [b]Refinery[/b] and the Iron Teeth in the [b]Grease Factory[/b].
